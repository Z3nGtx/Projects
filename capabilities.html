End-to-End Manufacturing Capabilities for Electrical & Switchgear Components

Manufacturing Capabilities

RK Enterprises offers comprehensive manufacturing capabilities focused on precision brazing, welding, and assembly for the electrical and switchgear industry. Our controlled processes and in-house facilities enable us to meet stringent OEM requirements.

Silver Brazing

Controlled silver brazing processes to achieve high joint strength, excellent conductivity, and consistent metallurgical quality.

Flame Brazing

Flame brazing of electrical components using approved silver alloys for reliable performance and durability.

CO₂ Welding

Strong and precise welding operations for mechanical assemblies used in electrical panel and switchgear applications.

Mechanical Assembly

Accurate assembly of latch, interlock, CAM, and arc deflector components as per customer specifications.

In-House Plating

Plating operations to improve corrosion resistance, surface finish, and service life of brazed components.

Quality Control

Incoming, in-process, and final inspections supported by documentation and traceability.

About RK Enterprises

Trusted Precision Brazing Partner for Electrical & Switchgear Industry

Who We Are

RK Enterprises is a Sheet metal and Manufacturing company located at MIDC Ahmednagar. We specialize in silver brazed, flame brazed and plated assemblies for electrical and switchgear applications.

With a strong focus on quality, reliability, and process control, we support OEMs and engineering companies by delivering components that meet strict technical and safety requirements.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality brazed assemblies through controlled processes, continuous improvement, and customer-focused manufacturing solutions.

Our Vision

To become a preferred and trusted brazing partner for electrical and switchgear OEMs in India and international markets.

Our Core Values

Quality

Strict adherence to ISO standards and controlled manufacturing processes.

Reliability

Consistent product performance and dependable delivery commitments.

Integrity

Transparent business practices and long-term customer relationships.

Improvement

Continuous process and quality improvement initiatives.

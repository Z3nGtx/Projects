Commitment to Quality, Compliance, and Continuous Improvement

Quality Policy

RK Enterprises is committed to delivering high-quality brazed and assembled components that meet customer requirements and applicable standards.

Our quality management system is designed to ensure process consistency, product reliability, and continuous improvement across all manufacturing activities.

  • Compliance with ISO quality standards
  • Controlled brazing and plating processes
  • Customer-focused quality objectives
  • Continuous process improvement

Quality Assurance Process

Incoming Inspection

Verification of raw materials as per specifications.

In-Process Control

Monitoring brazing parameters and assembly processes.

Final Inspection

Dimensional, visual, and functional inspection before dispatch.

Documentation

Process records, inspection reports, and traceability.

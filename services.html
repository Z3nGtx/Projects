We manufacture high-precision arc deflector assemblies using controlled brazing processes. These assemblies are used in MCBs, MCCBs, and switchgear systems to manage electrical arcs and enhance operational safety.

  • Consistent brazing quality
  • OEM-compliant processes

Latch & Interlock Assemblies

We supply latch and interlock assemblies for electrical panel control systems, ensuring mechanical reliability and operational safety.

  • Precision assembly
  • Functional reliability
  • Application-specific designs

Silver Brazed & Plated CAM Assemblies

Our silver brazed and plated CAM assemblies are designed for electrical and switchgear mechanisms, offering durability, corrosion resistance, and long service life.

  • Dimensional accuracy

  • Suitable for industrial and commercial seating systems
